Search Agile Word

September 17, 2020

To be honest this google books chart bothers me, since the 1990s, including the word agile in books has been increasing. Some argue this is great news, a sign that it is popular and resonating. Or that it’s been a boon for self-publishing books with the rise of the Internet.

I’d argue this is a problem, a sign that the word is misunderstood and often redefined, leading to a lack of clarity. If my argument is true and you decide to make a significant organisational change based on this word. How successful do you think that would that be?

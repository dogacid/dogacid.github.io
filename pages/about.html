About

I'm a consultant who likes to help leaders, groups and organisations work towards productive outcomes, identify systematic issues and appropriate action oriented learning in place.

I've worked heavily with Technology groups, Customer facing teams and Leadership across many types of organisations and industries.

Starting out as a Bank Officer in the late 1980's then as Software Technical Lead by the dot com boom, I moved to Sydney and worked for the successful fintech tyro payments as a team of founding engineers until settling at ThoughtWorks Australia.

I spend my time researching the theories and methods that are effective in changing individuals, customers, teams, and organisations. Looking to other knowledge fields for insights to improve my capability.

Following the work of Edgar H. Schein, J. Richard Hackman, Chris Argyris, W. Edward Deming, John Seddon, Roger Schwarz, Bob Moesta, and the psychoanalytic community.

I've actively founded two meet-up groups in Perth and Melbourne:

  • Agile-Perth
  • #JTBD Melbourne

I've presented at Agile Australia, various meet-ups, and presented at these events:

  • Product Camp
  • LAST Conference
